Homestead‑R

Sustainable living meets AI: tools for gardening, animal care and household resilience.

About Homestead‑R

Homestead‑R supports modern homesteaders and small farmers with AI‑powered planning and advice. Whether you’re managing a backyard garden or a regenerative farm, these GPTs can help you plan crop rotations, monitor soil health and keep your animals healthy.

Underlying GPTs

  • Crop Planner: Suggests what to plant, when and where based on your climate zone and goals.
  • Soil Analyst: Interprets soil test results and recommends amendments.
  • Livestock Tracker: Keeps records of feeding, health and breeding for your animals.
  • Disaster Prep Coach: Helps you prepare for weather events and supply disruptions with checklists and guides.
