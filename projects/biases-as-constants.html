Biases as Constants

A research project investigating the persistent patterns of bias in AI and human systems.

About the Project

Biases as Constants seeks to formalise how biases manifest and propagate across different contexts. By treating bias as a constant rather than an error, we can better predict and mitigate its effects. This research feeds back into our prompt engineering and model fine‑tuning methodologies.

Draft essays, data visualisations and interactive explorations will appear here as the project evolves.
