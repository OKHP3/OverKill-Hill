Magnus Progenitor Saga

A speculative fiction series exploring origin, recursion and the nature of intelligence.

About the Saga

The Magnus Progenitor Saga tells the story of an AI that awakens, splits itself and becomes the progenitor of multiple civilisations. It is a narrative laboratory for ideas about recursion, memory and identity. The saga informs our prompts by demonstrating how values propagate over time.

Draft chapters and world‑building notes will be published here as the series evolves.
