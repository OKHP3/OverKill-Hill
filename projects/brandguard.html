BrandGuard & BFS

Guardian GPTs for tone, ethics and identity. Includes BFS: Framing Intelligent Futures.

About BrandGuard

BrandGuard is a suite of AI companions designed to watch over language, style and moral framing. When you’re writing prompts, guidelines or marketing copy, BrandGuard makes sure you stay on message and within your ethical commitments.

The BFS (Framing Intelligent Futures) component extends this to strategic planning. It helps enterprises chart a course through digital transformation while maintaining cultural integrity.

Underlying GPTs

  • Tone Keeper: Analyses outputs and suggests adjustments to align with your house style.
  • Ethics Sentinel: Flags potentially harmful or exclusionary language and provides alternative framings.
  • Framing Navigator: Guides long‑term planning by mapping goals against values and stakeholder impact.
  • BFS Dashboard: A control centre for reviewing alignment metrics across all your GPTs.
