Abrahamic Reference Engine

A semantic index across the texts and traditions of Judaism, Christianity and Islam.

Work in Progress

The Abrahamic Reference Engine is an early experiment in building a multi‑faith concordance. It aims to link names, places, concepts and narratives across the Torah, Bible and Quran, highlighting common ground and respectful distinctions.

Once complete, users will be able to ask comparative questions and receive responses that are sensitive to context, tradition and audience.
