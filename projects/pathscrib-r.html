PathScrib‑R

A narrative copilot and generative writing suite. Explore, draft and iterate on your stories with AI assistance.

About PathScrib‑R

PathScrib‑R is built for authors, game masters and world builders. It helps you brainstorm plot points, refine characters and experiment with narrative structure. With a flexible Copilot Agent you can delegate tasks like outlining, summarising and even converting text into storyboards.

Copilot & GPT Components

  • Plot Weaver: Suggests possible turns and twists based on your genre and themes.
  • Character Smith: Generates nuanced character sheets and backstories.
  • Arc Visualiser: Converts a synopsis into a simple plot arc diagram.
  • Copilot Agent: A multi‑step agent that takes user instructions and executes them across the PathScrib‑R suite.
