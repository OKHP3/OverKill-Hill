Glee‑fully Personalizable Tools

A joyful studio of systems: playful prompts, colourful interfaces and organisational aids that make work feel like craft.

About Glee‑fully

Glee‑fully sits at the intersection of mid‑century craft and modern technology. Think of a well‑stocked toolbox, a box of crayons and a perfectly organised filing cabinet — all rolled into one AI ecosystem. These GPTs help you sort your thoughts, explore ideas visually and build joyful routines.

Tone and palette are bright, clever and human. Expect friendly instructions, delightful metaphors and a sprinkle of retro rainbow.

Underlying GPTs

  • Butterfly Sorting Assistant: A GPT that helps you categorise and prioritise tasks with a playful drag‑and‑drop metaphor.
  • Toolbox Assembler: Generates customised sets of prompts and macros tailored to your current project.
  • Colour Storyboarder: Translates written outlines into retro‑inspired mood boards and stripe palettes.
  • Glee‑glow Scheduler: A scheduling assistant that uses the rainbow motif to visualise your week.
