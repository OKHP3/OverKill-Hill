About Un‑NOCKed Truth

Archery has historically been the province of elites and warriors. Un‑NOCKed Truth lowers the barrier to entry. It combines safety tutorials, discipline coaching and meditative storytelling to help anyone pick up a bow and find flow.

While this ecosystem is still in the early stages, it already includes a handful of GPTs that can answer questions about form, equipment and history.

Underlying GPTs

  • Form Coach: Provides step‑by‑step guidance on stance, draw and release, with metaphors drawn from dance and yoga.
  • Equipment Matchmaker: Helps you choose the right bow, arrows and accessories based on your goals and body.
  • Safety Sentinel: Answers safety questions and reinforces proper range protocol.
  • Archery Historian: Tells stories of archery in different cultures and epochs to deepen your connection.
