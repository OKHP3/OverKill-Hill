v0.3 — The Visual Edition
The slide deck is now live.

The Council produced the diagrams. The deck packages the field. The polls let the audience score the work. That matters because the whole ROY argument depends on a simple question:

Did the diagram create enough clarity to justify the words that produced it?

The v0.3 release turns that question into a public test.

The deck is my first read. The polls are the audience read. The final debrief is where the two get compared.
