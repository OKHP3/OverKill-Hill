About OverKill Hill P³

OverKill Hill P³ is the bridge between lumber yards and language models, between spreadsheets and storyboards, between “we should automate this” and “we trust what we’ve built.”

Who’s on the Hill

OverKill Hill P³ is led by Jamie — a lumber and building products veteran who spent years with boots on actual concrete and sawdust on actual floors before stepping into AI prompt architecture.

That means the work here is grounded in the realities of physical business: margins, logistics, human constraints and the way decisions actually get made when the clock is ticking.

Why the Hill exists

This is the place for people who don’t want “just a bot,” but a system that can carry part of the load without adding more chaos. The work is part engineering, part storytelling, part quietly protecting your future self from avoidable headaches.

How we work with you

Projects usually start with a conversation — context, constraints, the actual stakes. From there we sketch out the protocols, build a prototype, and then iterate until it behaves the way your world needs it to.

Sometimes that looks like a single, sharp-edged tool. Sometimes it’s a whole stack that touches multiple roles or departments. Either way, it’s built to last.

Ready to talk? Reach out at contact@overkillhill.com and we’ll start sketching.
