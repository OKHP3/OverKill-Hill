    Systems & Ecosystems

    • Glee‑fully Personalizable Tools: A retro‑modern toolbox of GPTs built for joy, organisation and creative play.
    • Ask Jamie: A mid‑century helpdesk persona with several specialist spin‑offs for documentation, technical coaching and more.
    • BrandGuard & BFS: Guardian GPTs to enforce tone, consistency and ethical framing, including the BFS: Framing Intelligent Futures strategic tool.
    • Un‑NOCKed Truth: Archery for anyone: a beginner‑friendly archery learning suite built on AI.
    • PathScrib‑R: A narrative copilot and generative writing assistant that explores plot and character arcs.
    • Homestead‑R: Homestead‑oriented GPTs for sustainable living, from seed planning to livestock care.
    • Found‑Rᵧ: GPTs that build GPTs. This meta‑ecosystem explores recursive design and emergent behaviour.
    • Work in progress:
      • Abrahamic Reference Engine: A semantic engine for interfaith textual exploration.
      • Magnus Progenitor Saga: An epic fiction project exploring origin and destiny.
      • Biases as Constants: A project interrogating how bias manifests across AI systems.
