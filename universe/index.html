Blueprint of the Hill

OverKill Hill P³™ sits at the center of a network of sibling brands, experimental GPT stacks and narrative worlds. This map shows how those layers are wired together — the forge, the friendly faces and the deliberately weird experiments.

How to read this map

Each node is a pillar in the OverKill Hill P³™ stack. Some pieces are production‑ready, others are active experiments. From an OKH perspective, this is the governance layer: what’s live, what’s in R&D and how the sibling brands plug into the spine.

OverKill Hill P³™

The engineering spine. This is where protocol patterns, canon and governance for GPT ecosystems live — the place we define how tools behave under ambiguity, load and real‑world constraints.

AskJamie™

The voice layer. A mid‑century helpdesk persona and its spin‑offs (BrandGuard™, BFS, and more) that narrate, clarify and walk people through decisions across the wider system.

Glee‑fully Tools™

The joy layer. A retro‑bright ecosystem of personalizable GPTs for everyday life — career, food, travel, health and identity — all built on OverKill Hill P³™ architecture.

Systems & ecosystems

Most of the universe starts at the Hill, then branches into branded experiences or narrative sandboxes. Here’s what’s live now, and what’s explicitly in “work in progress” territory.

  • Glee‑fully Personalizable Tools™: A retro‑modern toolbox of GPTs built for joy, organization and creative play.
  • AskJamie™: A mid‑century helpdesk persona with several specialist spin‑offs for documentation, technical coaching and more.
  • OKH P³ BrandGuard™: Guardian GPTs to enforce tone, consistency and ethical framing, including the BFS: Framing Intelligent Futures strategic tool.
  • Un‑NOCKed Truth: Archery for anyone: a beginner‑friendly archery learning suite built on AI.
  • PathScrib‑R: A narrative copilot and generative writing assistant that explores plot and character arcs.
  • Homestead‑R: Homestead‑oriented GPTs for sustainable living, from seed planning to livestock care.
  • Found‑Rᵧ: GPTs that build GPTs. This meta‑ecosystem explores recursive design and emergent behavior.
  • Work in progress:
    • Abrahamic Reference Engine: A semantic engine for interfaith textual exploration.
    • Magnus Progenitor Saga: An epic fiction project exploring origin and destiny.
    • Biases as Constants: A project interrogating how bias manifests across AI systems.
