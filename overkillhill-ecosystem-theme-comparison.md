## Where They Diverge

### 1. Mode — Dark vs Light

OverKill Hill is a **dark-first** site. It is the forge: heavy, industrial, authoritative. Visitors arrive at night, at depth.

Glee-fully and AskJamie are both **light-first** sites. They are the daylight products that come out of the forge. Approachable, warm, open.

This is the most significant visual divergence. Dark espresso backgrounds vs cream paper is not a styling choice — it communicates hierarchy and personality.

---

### 2. Primary CTA Colour Temperature

| Site | CTA | Temperature | Emotion |
|---|---|---|---|
| OverKill Hill | `#c46a2c` → `#e6a03c` | Warm fire | Confident, earned, forged |
| Glee-fully | `#d35b2d` → `#f3b932` | Warm fire (brighter) | Energetic, inviting, joyful |
| AskJamie | `#2d6f7e` → `#3c8ea1` | Cool water | Calm, trustworthy, personal |

OKH and Glee-fully share a fire-spectrum CTA family. AskJamie breaks from this entirely into cool teal — deliberately. AskJamie is the human interface; its colour says "I listen" rather than "I forge."

Glee-fully's CTA is a brighter, more saturated version of the OKH CTA — more cheerful, less heavy. Both use diagonal gradients (OKH at 135°, Glee at 90° — Glee's is more horizontal/flowing, OKH's is more angular/dynamic).

---

### 3. Display Typography — The Strongest Brand Signal

This is where the three brands most clearly separate:

| Site | Heading Font | Character |
|---|---|---|
| OverKill Hill | **Alfa Slab One** | Heavy industrial slab serif. Stamp. Authority. No softness. |
| Glee-fully | **Fredoka** | Rounded, playful. Bubbly weight. Joy is visible in the letterforms. |
| AskJamie | **Baloo 2 + Kalam** | Baloo 2 = warm rounded. Kalam = handwritten accent. Personal, human, intimate. |

All three sites share a clean humanist sans (DM Sans or Open Sans) for body text — the voice is consistent. The display font is where the personality lives.

Alfa Slab One says: *this was built in a machine shop.*
Fredoka says: *this was made for you with care.*
Baloo 2 + Kalam says: *hi, I'm Jamie.*

## Summary: What Each Brand Communicates

**OverKill Hill P³™** — You have arrived at the source. Everything here was made with precision. The darkness is intentional. The weight is earned. This is the forge where the tools are made.

**Glee-fully Tools™** — The tools are ready for you. Light, warm, accessible. The joy is built in — not painted on. The cream and gold and rounded type say: this was made to be used by real people, happily.

**AskJamie™** — I'm here. Tell me what you need. The teal is calm. The cream is familiar. The handwritten Kalam accent is a signature, not a brand mark. This site has a person behind it and wants you to feel that.
